About Us

Broadway English Academy is dedicated to empowering students with effective communication skills, professional fluency, and the confidence to succeed in global careers. We believe in quality education, student engagement, and lifelong learning.

Our experienced instructors focus on spoken English, academic development, and job-ready communication to help learners navigate their future successfully.

Our Courses

Spoken English

Enhance your fluency, pronunciation, and confidence in everyday conversations and interviews.

Academic English

Develop strong reading, writing, and grammar skills for academic and competitive exams.

Job Preparation

Learn resume writing, email etiquette, and interview strategies for the corporate world.

Children's English Programs

Our Methodology

  • Interactive Classes: Focused on real-world communication skills.
  • Level-Based Learning: Beginner to advanced courses tailored to your progress.
  • Audio-Visual Aids: Smart tools and practical materials used in every class.
  • Assessment & Feedback: Regular practice, mock tests, and teacher reviews.
  • Student Engagement: Role plays, debates, public speaking, and group activities.
